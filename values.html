Codeup's Core Values

These are extremely important to us. These core values are the foundation of how we do business, how we conduct ourselves and how we interact with each other. We look to these core values as guidance when making decisions and developing strategies to fulfill our mission.

What do these values mean to you?

Be Helpful

To me, being helpful means always doing my best to help others who may not be as fortunate.

Cultivate Inclusive Growth

I believe that everyone has the power to change their own life, but sometimes, people just need a little help. You can both give a man a fish and teach a man to fish. They're not mutually exclusive, and it's easier to learn how to fish if you're not already starving.

Deliver Excellence

Delivering excellence means that nothing I work on will ever be just "good enough". Delivering excellence means to uphold the highest standards of performance from both myself and my team.

Act with Integrity

Acting with integrity means always doing the right thing, whether or not it is easy or convenient. Acting with integrity is an essential component of delivering excellence, as it is an unwavering adherence to the highest standards and principles.
